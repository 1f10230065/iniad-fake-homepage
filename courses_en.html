Four courses of INIAD

  • INIAD Engineering Course
    • Develop the ability to support collaboration from a technical perspective and to materialize new information services.
  • INIAD Design Course
    • To support collaboration from the design aspect, and to develop the ability to materialize new information services and products.
  • INIAD Business Course
    • Develop managerial talent capable of building new businesses through collaboration.
  • INIAD Civil System Course
    • Develop the ability to apply information technology to the creation of an affluent society and lifestyle.
